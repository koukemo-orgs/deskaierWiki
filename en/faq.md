# FAQ

## Q. How can I hide specific services from AI Quick Launch?
A. Go to Settings > Platform > AI quick launch priority and toggle visibility per service.

## Q. Can I assign icons to web shortcuts?
A. Yes. You can choose an icon in the add/edit web shortcut dialog.

## Q. Restore finished but settings did not apply
A. Restart the app after restoring.
